Solar Energy Potential

Exploring the power of the sun through real-world data and energy concepts.

About This Project

This project explores how solar energy can be used as a renewable and sustainable power source. Using principles of energy conversion and transport, we analyze how sunlight can be captured and converted into usable electricity through photovoltaic cells.

Solar Energy by Location

Curious about how much sunlight your city gets? Try checking the average solar irradiance data for your area using tools like Global Solar Atlas.

Scientific Concepts

  • Energy Transport: Sunlight carries electromagnetic energy that can be absorbed by solar panels.
  • Energy Conversion: Photovoltaic cells convert light energy into electrical energy.
  • Efficiency: The conversion efficiency depends on panel materials and sunlight intensity.

Reflection

By understanding solar energy potential, we can better design systems that reduce reliance on fossil fuels and support a sustainable energy future. This project connects to course objectives by analyzing energy transport, conversion, and comparison between renewable and nonrenewable sources.
